Privacy Policy — Zalary

Last updated: September 04, 2025

Short version: Zalary does not collect, share, or sell your personal data. All information you enter in the app remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Who we are

Developer/Publisher: Kalidhasan Devaguru

App: Zalary (Android)

Contact: kalidhasandevaguru@gmail.com

Data we handle

When you use Zalary, you may enter information such as your salary, work hours, allowances, and other calculation inputs. This information is used solely to perform on-device calculations and display results within the app.

Categories
  • Salary and compensation inputs you type
  • App settings and preferences
Purpose
  • Provide features (e.g., salary breakdown, take-home pay estimation)
  • Save your preferences so you don’t have to re-enter them

Processing & storage

  • On-device only: Your data is stored locally on your Android device (e.g., in app preferences). It is not uploaded to us.
  • No third-party analytics/ads SDKs: The app does not include advertising or analytics libraries.
  • No background data transfer: Zalary does not send your data to external servers.

Permissions

Zalary does not request sensitive Android permissions (e.g., Contacts, Location, Camera, Microphone). If future versions add permissions, we will update this policy and ask for your consent where required.

Data retention & deletion

  • Retention: Data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Deletion: You can clear app data from Android settings at any time. If Zalary includes a “Reset data” option in settings, using it will remove app-stored data.

Children’s privacy

Zalary is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

International users

Because Zalary processes data only on your device and does not transmit it to our servers, there is no cross-border transfer of your personal information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect app changes or legal requirements. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after an update indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
